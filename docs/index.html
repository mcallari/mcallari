Why A Distributed Architecture?

Devices, Time, and Teams

Having a scaleable infrastructure is more than adding servers. It is having the ability to make your business available on multiple devices all working off of a centeralized data model.

Deploying a RESTful API service allows you to have your web, mobile, and os teams to keep their concentration on delivering the best user experience on those devices. The data team provides the consistency needed for the business to flourish across all devices.

Great Ideas Need Great Operations

Implementing new technology to either support your business or launch a new product without the proper planning and procedures can derail any project.

At three-x-square we begin with a proper budget analyisis for the propsosed project, which informs the rest of the operations plan for project management, design, coding, testing, and deployments.

Repeateable processes allow for proper analyisis to make tangible decisions on how to deploy your technology to grow your business.
